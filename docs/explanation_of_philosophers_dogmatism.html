main

this can also be phrased as a

problem of philosophical progress

consider the following claims (inspired by were_right_theyre_wrong)

  1. epistemic peers
    • rough equality of intelligence/cognitive resources
    • roughly equal access to evidence
  2. no further evidence discoverable in future not discoverable now
  3. widespread disagreement among philosophers
  4. philosophy tends to make progress
