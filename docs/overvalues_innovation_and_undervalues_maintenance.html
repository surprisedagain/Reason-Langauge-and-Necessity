main

There is the more or less normative sense of over-value. Places more premium/reward/fame on producing a novel idea/object/practice than produces a desirable/optimal/correct level of innovation.

But there is also a sense of simply over-estimating the causal influence of innovators and under-estimating the influence of adopters.
