A first rate grant applcation maker who mistook himself for a first rate philosopher and used his social position to require his adherents to make the same mistake.

Has some interesting points about time. Questions excellent, suggestion of backwards causation tantalising. But his answers to these questions are by and large ludicrous.

Anyone who asks us to take seriously the notion that certain regions on the universe may be running backwards in time has lost contact with their sense of reality.
